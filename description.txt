Pocket Nurse adds a new PostMech accessory that allows you to heal for price of  2 silver per hp.
It will also grant you some buffs for short amount of time.
You can craft it at Mythril Anvil with:
- 15x Hallowed Bar
- 30x Greater Healing Potion
- 5x Soul of Light
You can also edit hotkey for healing inside of game
Default hotkey is 'h'
